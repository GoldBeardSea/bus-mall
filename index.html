Hello and welcome to Bus Mall. We a new and exciting start up that offers users the ability to read through a catalog with new, unique, and exciting products from different local artists, companies, and knick knackeries. We are still in our testing phase and we need information from you, our trusted consumers. You will see three images displayed on your screen. Please select the product that you most like out of the three. At the end we will produce a chart for you to see offering. You can reset the page, by either navigating the page or refreshing. However, you scores will be kept to build a progressive series of votes. Every 25 votes it will end functionality and produce a chart. Thank you for your input.
